# Founder Pitch: Autonomous Multi-Platform Agent

---

**Hello, I am Sesha and I have built an Autonomous Multi-Platform Agent System.**

---

## The Problem

Every support team's morning looks the same: open Jira, check unassigned tickets. Switch to Slack, scroll through overnight messages. Log into Salesforce, update customer records. Open the CRM, verify nothing fell through the cracks.

**This is broken.**

- Teams spend 2-3 hours daily just *monitoring* systems—not solving problems, just looking for them
- Critical issues sit unnoticed for hours
- Customer escalations happen because someone didn't see an alert
- High-priority tickets get buried
- Context gets lost jumping between platforms

The cost isn't just time. It's the customer who churned because their issue took 48 hours instead of 4. It's the deal that slipped because Salesforce wasn't updated. It's the team burnout from constant context-switching.

---

## What I Built

An autonomous agent that continuously scans all connected platforms (Jira, Slack, Salesforce, local databases), identifies unresolved issues, proposes solutions, and executes them automatically—generating a full report when complete.

No more manual checking. No more missed alerts. The agent works 24/7, catches what humans miss, and takes action before problems escalate.

---

## How It Works

- **Scans** all connected platforms every few seconds
- **Detects** unassigned tickets, missing data, overdue tasks, sync failures
- **Routes** issues to the correct handler based on intelligent scoring
- **Executes** solutions automatically (assign, alert, sync, notify)
- **Reports** everything that was found and fixed
